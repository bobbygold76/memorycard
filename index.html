How to Play

1

The goal is to find all matching pairs of cards.

2

Click on a card to flip it over and reveal its symbol.

3

Click on another card to find its match.

4

If the two cards match, they will stay face up.

5

If they don't match, they will flip back over.

6

In multiplayer mode, players take turns trying to find matches.

7

The player with the most matches wins!
